Safety Protocols

This section contains important safety protocols and procedures for all maritime operations.

(Content to be implemented. This page should be available offline if cached for quick reference.)

General Safety:

  • Always wear appropriate PPE (Hard Hat, Steel-Toed Boots, High-Visibility Vest).
  • Report any unsafe conditions or incidents immediately to your supervisor.
  • Be aware of moving vehicles and machinery.
  • No smoking in non-designated areas.

Cargo Handling:

  • Ensure loads are secure before lifting.
  • Follow proper lifting techniques.
  • Understand hazard symbols for dangerous goods.

Emergency Procedures:

  • Fire Evacuation: Proceed to nearest assembly point (see map).
  • Man Overboard: Alert supervisor, throw lifebuoy, keep visual contact.
  • Medical Emergency: Contact port medical services (see Emergency Contacts).
